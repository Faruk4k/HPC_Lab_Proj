Assignment 1:
-------------

 - Thread version: dot_product (parallel_v1), false_sharing, 
 - OpenMP version: simple_triad

- Core range evaluation : [1, 2, 4, 8, 16]

  - 'Fwd_GETS::total' (request for sharing read)
